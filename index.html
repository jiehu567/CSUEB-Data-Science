CSUEB Data Science Group

Welcome to Data Science at Cal State East Bay Statistics Webpage. This is a project that is put together by a group of passionate students and professors exploring and innovating in the field of statistics through the use of newly developed frameworks and technologies in Data Science and Big Data. Among the goals set out is the goal to bring statistics and statisticians closer to the use of new technologies that have recently come to light. Specifically Hadoop and Big Data.
