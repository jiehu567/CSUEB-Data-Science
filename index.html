Welcome to Big Data and Data Science at CSUEB

Welcome to the Big Data and Data Science at Cal State East Bay Statistics Webpage. This is a project that is put together by a group of passionate students and professors exploring and innovating in the field of statistics through the use of big data data data science. Among the goals set out is the goal to bring statistics and statisticians closer to the use of new technologies that have recently come to light. Specifically Hadoop and Big Data as well as other Data Science fundamentals and frameworks.
